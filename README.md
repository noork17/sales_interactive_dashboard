# Sales_dashboard
This project is part of my **Data Analyst Internship (Task 8)**.   The objective was to design a **basic interactive sales dashboard** using Power BI to analyze sales performance by **product, region, and month**.


## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard includes:
- **KPIs:** Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity  
- **Sales by Category & Sub-Category** (Bar Chart)  
- **Sales Trend by Month** (Line Chart)  
- **Sales by Region & Segment** (Matrix Table)  
- **On-time vs Delayed Shipping** (Donut Chart)  
- **Sales vs Profit** (Scatter Plot)  
- **Best Selling Products** (Bar Chart)  

---

##  Key Insights
1. **Sales Trend:** Sales peaked in April (~32K) and July (~27K), while March recorded the lowest (~11K).  
2. **Regional Performance:** South region achieved the highest sales (~4.44K), followed by the West (~4.23K).  
3. **Category Performance:** Technology (92K) slightly outperformed Furniture (90K) and Office Supplies (82K).  
4. **Customer Segment:** Corporate customers contributed significantly in the South (~2K sales).
